Help & Support

Need assistance? Check the FAQs below or reach out directly.

Windows Defender/SmartScreen is blocking the app. Why?

Since this is a new indie software without an expensive digital certificate, Windows may flag it as an "Unknown Publisher". The software is 100% safe. Simply click on "More info" and then "Run anyway" to start the application.

Where do my downloaded files get saved?

By default, your videos and audio files are saved in your computer's Downloads folder.You can change the destination folder anytime from the New Download tab in the app.

Is this software truly free?

Yes! Neeraj YT Downloader is completely free to use. There are no hidden subscription fees, no locked features, and no annoying advertisements.

My download stopped midway. Do I have to start over?

Not at all. The software supports a full Resume feature. If your internet disconnects, simply use the resume button and it will continue downloading exactly from where it stopped.
